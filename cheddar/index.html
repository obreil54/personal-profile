Project Overview

Built with Ruby on Rails, Stimulus.js, Capybara, and Selenium, Cheddar streamlines the job application process by automating form submissions. Users input their details once, and the system intelligently fills out job applications, ensuring accuracy and efficiency.

Developed as part of a two-week bootcamp project, Cheddar demonstrates my ability to build and deploy scalable, automation-driven web applications. I led the development of its core automation feature, leveraging Selenium and Capybara to navigate and populate application forms dynamically.

On the front end, I integrated external APIs to fetch and display company and university logos, enhancing visual engagement and real-time data interaction. Hosted on Heroku, the platform reflects my commitment to agile development and solving real-world problems through automation.
